The Life of Rugor Nass

The man who slobbered across the galaxy

Here's a timeline of Boss Nass' life in Star Wars:

  • 32 BBY - Waived Jar Jar's death sentence to allow him to fulfill his life debt to Qui-Gon by being their navigator.
  • 21 BBY - Rugor Nass was no longer leader of the gungans and was replaced by Lyonie.
  • 19 BBY - Had resumed his position of Boss and agreed to aid Freus Olin by lending Gungan aid to Naboo troops to attack and destroy Imperial headquarters on Naboo. Despite his disdain for the Empire he represented them during Empire Day in Theed.
  • 1 ABY - Nass helped spacers find and destroy the super battle droid in order to finally recover the Old Repiblic droid module.
